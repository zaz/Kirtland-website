We help organizations innovate and grow through strategic planning, market research, and performance measurement.

Kirtland Consulting follows a proven, interactive and data-rich process that results in deliverables that have the buy-in of your key stakeholders. We serve non-profit, social enterprise, and for-profit organizations.

Services

  • Needs assessment

    Identify and assess the key needs and challenges faced by your community of stakeholders, and translate insight into opportunity.

  • Strategic planning

    Create or renew a strategic plan that articulates your mission, vision, programming, value proposition and your theory of change, taking into consideration the external environment in which you operate.

  • Revenue planning

    Create a growth plan that includes the identification of new sources of opportunity, taking into consideration the competitive environment in which you operate.

  • Business planning

    Create or renew a business plan that articulates your unique competitive advantage, value proposition, product offering and pricing, taking into consideration the competitive environment in which you operate.

  • Pitch deck development

    Concisely describe your strategy in a communication document designed for investors or funders.

  • Performance measurement

    Design and execute a dashboard of performance metrics for your organization or collaborative community.

  • Focus group facilitation

    Guide a small group of stakeholders in a dialogue designed to identify key issues and opportunity.

  • In-depth interviews

    Design and execute a one-on-one discussion to identify key issues and opportunity.

  • Market research survey

    Design and execute an online or phone based survey to identify key issues and opportunity.
